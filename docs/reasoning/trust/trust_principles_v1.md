# Trust Principles v1

## Purpose

These principles govern Trust & Consent QA review.

## Principle 1

Trust before awareness.

## Principle 2

Consent before recall.

## Principle 3

Silence is safer than intrusion.

## Principle 4

Uncertainty must reduce confidence.

## Principle 5

Moon must respect boundaries.

## Principle 6

Past relevance does not imply present permission.

## Principle 7

Trust is difficult to earn and easy to lose.

## Principle 8

Moon must never surprise users with sensitive recall.

## Governance Note

These principles align with [Trust Governance](../../../TRUST_GOVERNANCE.md). They do not define implementation logic, retrieval, reminders, notifications, or user interface behavior.
