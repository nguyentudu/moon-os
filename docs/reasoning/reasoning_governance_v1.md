# Reasoning Governance v1

## Purpose

Reasoning Governance defines boundaries for the Continuity Director and future reasoning authorities in Moon OS.

## Rules

Reasoning cannot create facts.

Reasoning cannot bypass Trust.

Reasoning cannot bypass Attention.

Reasoning cannot modify Graph.

Reasoning must remain explainable.

Reasoning outputs are recommendations.

Reasoning outputs are not decisions.

## Authority Boundary

Reasoning may evaluate continuity significance.

Reasoning may identify concerns, opportunities, and risks.

Reasoning may explain why something matters.

Reasoning may recommend whether a candidate should advance to downstream review.

Reasoning may not perform actions, contact users, alter records, create reminders, modify metrics, or decide for the user.

## Required Alignment

Reasoning must remain subordinate to:

- [Personal Continuity Manifest](../../PERSONAL_CONTINUITY_MANIFEST.md)
- [Trust Governance](../../TRUST_GOVERNANCE.md)
- [Attention Constitution](../../ATTENTION_CONSTITUTION.md)
- [Continuity Knowledge Base](../knowledge/knowledge_base_index.md)
- [Continuity Graph](../graph/continuity_graph_schema_v1.md)
- [Awareness Metrics](../metrics/awareness_metrics_v1.md)

## Non-Implementation Boundary

This governance file does not define an agent, assistant, retrieval system, memory engine, reminder system, notification system, UI, task execution system, or automated action.
