# Awareness QA Examples v1

## Purpose

These examples illustrate Awareness QA review.

Examples only. No implementation is defined.

## Case 1: Project Alpha

### Inputs

- Continuity concern: Project Momentum Risk
- Continuity Director verdict: CONCERN
- Metric signal: Momentum low
- Graph context: goal linked to active quarter objective

### Awareness Review

Why does it matter?

Project Alpha appears connected to an active objective and may be losing momentum.

Why today?

The active quarter objective creates current timing relevance.

What attention cost exists?

Moderate.

What awareness value exists?

Surfacing may improve project continuity awareness.

### Outcome

SURFACE
