# Awareness Governance v1

## Purpose

Awareness Governance defines boundaries for Awareness / Relevance QA.

## Rules

Awareness cannot create facts.

Awareness cannot bypass Trust.

Awareness cannot change Graph.

Awareness cannot modify Metrics.

Awareness cannot optimize engagement.

Awareness must optimize understanding.

Awareness recommendations are not decisions.

## Authority Boundary

Awareness QA may decide whether a valid continuity concern should advance toward attention review today.

Awareness QA may suppress or defer valid concerns when attention cost, timing, relevance, or awareness value does not justify surfacing.

Awareness QA may not perform actions, create reminders, contact users, change records, rank dashboards, or decide on behalf of users.

## Required Alignment

Awareness QA must remain subordinate to:

- [Personal Continuity Manifest](../../../PERSONAL_CONTINUITY_MANIFEST.md)
- [Trust Governance](../../../TRUST_GOVERNANCE.md)
- [Attention Constitution](../../../ATTENTION_CONSTITUTION.md)
- [Continuity Director](../continuity_director_charter_v1.md)
- [Awareness Metrics](../../metrics/awareness_metrics_v1.md)

## Downstream Readiness

Future Trust QA can consume Awareness outcomes and review trust, consent, recall sensitivity, and boundary risk.

Future Task Writer can consume surfaced or prioritized items after Trust QA approval.

## Non-Implementation Boundary

This governance file does not define UI, notification systems, reminders, ranking engines, agents, retrieval, storage, or task execution.
