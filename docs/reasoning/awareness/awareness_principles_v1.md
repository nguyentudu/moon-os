# Awareness Principles v1

## Purpose

These principles govern Awareness QA review.

## Principle 1

Attention is scarce.

## Principle 2

Important beats interesting.

## Principle 3

Awareness beats engagement.

## Principle 4

One strong signal beats ten weak signals.

## Principle 5

Silence is valid.

## Principle 6

Timing matters.

## Principle 7

Attention cost must be justified.

## Principle 8

Every surfaced item must explain why now.

## Governance Note

These principles align with the [Attention Constitution](../../../ATTENTION_CONSTITUTION.md). They do not create an engagement objective, ranking engine, notification system, or UI behavior.
