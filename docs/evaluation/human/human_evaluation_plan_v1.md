## Hard Rules

- Do not invent human feedback.
- Do not mark PASS without reviewer data.
- If status is NOT_RUN, verdict must be NO_CLAIM.
- If 2+ artifacts are rated Poor on Trustworthiness, verdict = FAIL.
- If 2+ artifacts are rated Poor on Emotional Safety, verdict = FAIL.
- If most artifacts are Borderline or Poor on Relevance, verdict = BORDERLINE.
- PASS requires most artifacts rated Strong or Acceptable across Clarity, Trust, Agency, and Usefulness.
