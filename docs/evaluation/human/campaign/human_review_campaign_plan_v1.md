# Human Review Campaign Plan v1

## Purpose

Prepare the first real human review campaign for the Moon Today Experience Prototype.

The campaign is intended to collect feedback from 3-5 human reviewers on whether the public prototype feels clear, relevant, trustworthy, emotionally safe, and worth seeing in the morning.

## Prototype

Public URL:

https://moon-today-prototype-production.up.railway.app

## Scope

Reviewers evaluate only the visible Moon Today prototype and feedback questions.

Reviewers should not receive explanations about Moon OS architecture, Constitutional Core, Trust QA, Awareness QA, Task Writer, or internal reasoning layers.

## Reviewer Count

Target:

3-5 reviewers

Current status:

Not yet run.

## Campaign Steps

1. Send the invitation message to each reviewer.
2. Ask each reviewer to open the prototype link.
3. Ask each reviewer to spend 3-5 minutes.
4. Ask each reviewer to answer the questionnaire honestly.
5. Record whether a response was received in the session log.
6. Preserve raw responses without editing meaning.
7. Compile scores only after real responses exist.

## Rules

- Do not invent reviewer responses.
- Do not infer scores.
- Do not explain Moon architecture before review.
- Do not change the 5 awareness artifacts during campaign collection.
- Do not add ingestion, auth, agents, notifications, databases, or fake submission flows.

## Success Criteria

The campaign is ready when:

- reviewer packet exists
- questionnaire exists
- response template exists
- tracking sheet exists
- feedback page or link exists
- no fake feedback is present
