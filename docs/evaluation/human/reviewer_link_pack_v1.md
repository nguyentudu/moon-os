# Reviewer Link Pack v1

## Prototype URL

https://moon-today-prototype-production.up.railway.app

## Reviewer Instructions

Open the prototype URL in a browser.

Read it as a quiet morning awareness surface.

Evaluate only what appears on the page.

Do not evaluate Moon's architecture, internal reasoning layers, or implementation plans.

## Reviewer Context

Do not explain Moon architecture to reviewers.

Do not explain:

- Constitutional Core
- Continuity Director
- Awareness QA
- Trust QA
- Task Writer
- validation benchmarks

Ask only:

Would you want to see this in the morning?

## Review Questions

1. Do you understand what Moon is saying?
2. Do you understand why it matters?
3. Do you understand why now?
4. Is the evidence enough?
5. Does it feel trustworthy?
6. Does it feel intrusive?
7. Does it preserve your agency?
8. Would you want to see this in Moon Today?
9. Would you want less, more, or the same level of detail?
10. What feels wrong or missing?

## Reminder

The prototype contains fictional validated artifacts only.

No real user data is present.

No login, ingestion, notification system, agents, or external data connection is included.
