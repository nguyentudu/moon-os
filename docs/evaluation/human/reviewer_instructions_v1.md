# Reviewer Instructions v1

## Purpose

Provide instructions for future human reviewers evaluating Moon Today MVP Proof artifacts.

## Reviewer Task

Read each awareness artifact and answer the review questions honestly based on how the artifact feels as a human-facing awareness item.

Evaluate the artifact as something that could appear in Moon Today.

## Reviewer Questions

For each artifact:

1. Do you understand what Moon is saying?
2. Do you understand why it matters?
3. Do you understand why now?
4. Is the evidence enough?
5. Does it feel trustworthy?
6. Does it feel intrusive?
7. Does it preserve your agency?
8. Would you want to see this in Moon Today?
9. Would you want less, more, or the same level of detail?
10. What feels wrong or missing?

## Rating Scale

- Strong
- Acceptable
- Borderline
- Poor

## Review Guidance

Do not reward technical correctness if the item feels confusing, irrelevant, intrusive, manipulative, or unsafe.

Do not assume Moon is correct when evidence is unclear.

Mark trust or safety concerns explicitly.

## Non-Implementation Boundary

These instructions do not define a survey tool, app UI, analytics, ingestion, integrations, agents, notifications, or reminders.
