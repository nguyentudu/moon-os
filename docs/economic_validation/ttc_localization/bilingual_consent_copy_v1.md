# Bilingual Consent Copy v1

## English

I consent to this study storing my copied/downloaded raw answers for later analysis.

## Tiếng Việt

Tôi đồng ý để study này lưu câu trả lời thô mà tôi copy/download cho phân tích sau này.

## Meaning Preservation

Both versions state that:

- the participant consents before starting
- raw answers may be stored
- storage occurs through copied or downloaded results
- analysis happens later

## Non-Claims

The consent copy does not claim:

- Moon improves Time To Context
- Moon saves time
- Moon has economic value
- participant answers will be scored immediately
