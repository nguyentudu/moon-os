# TTC Limitations v1

## Evidence Limitations

- Small sample: 6 participants.
- Language scope: all runs are Vietnamese.
- Mode assignment may not be randomized.
- No blinded answer quality scoring yet.
- No independent quality evaluator yet.
- Participant familiarity with Moon is not controlled.
- P-001 may reflect first-run friction or interruption.
- Decision Memory answers may reflect shared retrieval rather than independent understanding.
- Manual mode may include different effort levels or search strategies.
- No Time To Contribution test has been run.
- No Continuity Loop test has been run.

## Claim Boundaries

Allowed claim:

- EARLY_TTC_SIGNAL

Not allowed:

- ECONOMIC_EVIDENCE
- TEAM_CONTINUITY_VALIDATED
- PMF
- BUSINESS_CASE_READY

## Required Next Evidence

Next evidence should include:

- blinded answer quality review
- randomized or counterbalanced study assignment
- explicit quality rubric application
- Time To Contribution pilot
- Continuity Loop pilot
