# Study Design v1

## Purpose

The study asks participants to reason from Moon evidence into recommendations for novel cases.

Cases must require:

Retrieve

↓

Understand

↓

Reason

↓

Judge

## Participant Conditions

Participants may use Moon Decision Memory and available Moon docs as context.

Participants must answer in their own words and justify recommendations.

## Case Requirements

Every case must:

- require more than one source or concept
- ask for rationale
- ask for evidence
- ask for risks
- ask for recommendation or judgement
- avoid direct-answer questions

## Evaluation Flow

1. Participant receives case.
2. Participant retrieves relevant context.
3. Participant explains the reasoning chain.
4. Participant identifies tradeoffs and constraints.
5. Participant makes a recommendation.
6. Reviewer evaluates the answer using the rubric.

## Outcome Categories

Outcome A: Shared Retrieval only

Recommendation: replication or study redesign.

Outcome B: Shared Reasoning signal

Recommendation: expanded Shared Understanding Study.

Outcome C: Shared Judgement signal

Recommendation: Time To Contribution Pilot.

Outcome D: Shared Understanding validated

Recommendation: Team Continuity Pilot.

## Claim Boundary

The current study design can prepare evidence collection, but it cannot claim any outcome until real participant answers exist.
