# Limitations v1

## Study Setup Limitations

- No participants have been run yet.
- No answers have been collected.
- No reviewer scoring has occurred.
- Cases are designed by Moon's current authoring process and may contain framing bias.
- Direct-answer absence has been assessed by design intent, not external audit.
- Future participants may still rely heavily on retrieval.

## Evidence Limitations

Even if participants reach similar conclusions, the study must still distinguish:

- copied answer
- shared retrieval
- shared reasoning
- shared judgement
- shared understanding

## Claim Limitations

This study setup does not allow claims of:

- Shared Understanding confirmed
- Team Continuity validated
- economic value
- PMF

## Required Future Safeguards

- preserve raw participant answers
- blind reviewer scoring when possible
- separate evidence from interpretation
- require reviewer comments
- document disagreement
