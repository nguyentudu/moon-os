# Decision Recall Measurement v1

## Purpose

Measure whether participants can recall the important parts of a decision after using Moon.

## Recall Components

Participants should be able to recall:

- decision
- reason
- evidence
- consequence
- current validity

## Study Method

1. Give participant a decision question.
2. Allow assigned mode:
   - manual docs
   - Decision Memory assisted
3. Ask for an answer.
4. After a delay or distractor task, ask participant to restate:
   - what was decided
   - why
   - what evidence supports it
   - what would make it stale or reversible

## Metrics

- recall completeness
- evidence recall
- rationale recall
- current validity recall
- mistaken recall count

## Economic Interpretation

Decision Recall may matter economically when teams avoid repeated debate, repeated onboarding explanation, or accidental reversal of settled constraints.
