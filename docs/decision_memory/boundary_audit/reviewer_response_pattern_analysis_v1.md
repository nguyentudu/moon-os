# Reviewer Response Pattern Analysis v1

## Purpose

Analyze the response pattern that produced zero Not Decision labels.

## Pattern Summary

Across all 50 excerpts in Reliability Results:

- total classifications: 150
- Not Decision classifications: 0
- Unclear classifications: 15
- Decision classifications: 135

Across the 15 expected Not Decision excerpts:

- Decision classifications: 36/45
- Unclear classifications: 9/45
- Not Decision classifications: 0/45

## Expected Not Decision Subpatterns

### Classified Decision By All Three Reviewers

EV-023, EV-024, EV-025, EV-032, EV-034, EV-038, EV-049, EV-050

Likely interpretation:

Reviewers treated status notes, UI questions, mapping shorthand, metric candidates, and taxonomy entries as decision-like.

### Mixed Decision / Unclear

EV-026, EV-027, EV-028, EV-031, EV-042, EV-043, EV-047

Likely interpretation:

Some reviewers sensed ambiguity, especially around metrics, counts, review flags, and risk observations, but did not choose Not Decision.

## Evidence vs Hypothesis

Evidence:

- No Not Decision labels were submitted.
- Unclear was used as the alternative to Decision.
- Not Decision expected excerpts often drew comments like Chưa hiểu, tại sao, Không rõ ý đồ.

Hypothesis:

Reviewers may have understood the task as identifying whether an excerpt is related to a decision, rather than whether it is itself a decision.
