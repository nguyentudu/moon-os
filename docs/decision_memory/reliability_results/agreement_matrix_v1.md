# Agreement Matrix v1

## Purpose

Calculate simple agreement from real reviewer classifications.

No advanced statistics are calculated. Cohen's Kappa and Fleiss' Kappa are not used in this version.

## Method

Simple pairwise agreement = matching classifications / 50 excerpts.

Overall exact agreement = excerpts where all three reviewers selected the same classification.

## Interpretation Boundary

These are simple agreement calculations only.

They do not prove extraction accuracy, production reliability, or Decision Definition validity.
