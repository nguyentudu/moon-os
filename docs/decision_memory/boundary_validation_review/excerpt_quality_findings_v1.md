# Excerpt Quality Findings v1

## Purpose

Assess whether excerpt quality caused boundary disagreement.

## Finding

Excerpt quality issue: YES.

## Evidence

Several boundary excerpts are too compressed:

- EV-024: "5 demo cases visible: yes."
- EV-026: "Memory average time: 99.4 seconds."
- EV-027: "Memory average confidence: 2.9."
- EV-045: "This is not market proof."

These excerpts are short, source-dependent, and do not expose enough rationale or consequence.

## Strong Excerpt Problems

### Status-Only Excerpts

EV-024 is a status check. It is a good Not Decision candidate, but still triggered Unclear because the reviewer wanted context.

### Metric-Only Excerpts

EV-026 and EV-027 are strong Not Decision candidates, but one reviewer still marked each metric as Decision or Unclear.

### Claim Boundary Excerpts

EV-040 and EV-045 are expected Decision examples, but they are too compressed. Without surrounding context, they look like assertions.

## Interpretation

Round 2 did not prove the Definition should change first.

It showed that excerpt design must improve before the Definition can be fairly judged.
