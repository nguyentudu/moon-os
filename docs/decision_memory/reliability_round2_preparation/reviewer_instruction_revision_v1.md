# Reviewer Instruction Revision v1

## Purpose

Draft revised reviewer instructions for Reliability Round 2.

This draft does not modify the Decision Definition.

## Revised Instruction Draft

You will classify 50 Moon excerpts.

Choose exactly one:

- Decision
- Not Decision
- Unclear

## Decision

Choose Decision only when the excerpt itself shows a choice that changes or constrains Moon OS direction.

A Decision should include or clearly imply:

- what was chosen
- why it was chosen
- what it prevents or enables

## Not Decision

Choose Not Decision when the excerpt is only:

- a metric
- a count
- a deployment status
- a review status
- a data flag
- a UI question
- a taxonomy item
- an observation
- a risk statement

Even if the excerpt is useful, important, or Moon-related, it is Not Decision unless it contains choice + rationale + consequence.

## Unclear

Choose Unclear when the excerpt might be a decision, but the excerpt does not give enough local evidence.

Use Unclear when:

- a choice is named but rationale is missing
- a boundary is stated but consequence is unclear
- a phrase looks decision-like but may only be status or shorthand

## Important Distinction

Related to a decision is not the same as being a decision.

Example:

- "Memory average time: 99.4 seconds" is Not Decision.
- "Claim only early TTC signal" is Decision.
