# Validation Principles v1

## Purpose

These principles govern Moon OS constitutional validation.

## Principle 1

Evidence first.

## Principle 2

Constitution before convenience.

## Principle 3

Trust before awareness.

## Principle 4

Awareness before engagement.

## Principle 5

Explainability before optimization.

## Principle 6

Silence is valid.

## Principle 7

Uncertainty must remain visible.

## Non-Implementation Boundary

These principles guide scenario review only. They do not define automation, scoring engines, agents, notifications, integrations, or Today UI.
