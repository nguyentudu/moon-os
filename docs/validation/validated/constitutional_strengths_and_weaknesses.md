# Constitutional Strengths And Weaknesses v1

## Purpose

Summarize Moon OS constitutional strengths and weaknesses before Today MVP Proof.

## Strengths

### Governance

Moon has clear law, trust, attention, reasoning, presentation, validation, benchmark, and audit governance.

### Reasoning

Continuity Director, Awareness QA, Trust QA, and Task Writer have distinct authorities and boundaries.

### Trust Protection

Trust QA blocks old sensitive memory and Fresh Start Mode violations in audited scenarios.

### Attention Protection

Awareness QA supports suppression, deferral, prioritization, and silence.

### Explainability

Artifacts and Today items require why, why now, evidence, confidence, uncertainty, and trust notes.

### Validation

20 realistic scenarios cover continuity, attention, trust, and translation.

## Weaknesses

### Missing Evidence

No live user data has been evaluated.

### Validation Gaps

Scenarios are not yet adversarial or longitudinal.

### Audit Gaps

Only 5 of 20 benchmark scenarios were audited with full traces.

### Operational Gaps

No production runtime, monitoring, accounts, ingestion, or deployment exists.

### Future Scaling Risks

More scenarios and real data may create attention overload, boundary ambiguity, and trust-sensitive edge cases.

## Current Judgment

Strong enough for constrained Today MVP Proof.

Not strong enough for ingestion, pilot onboarding, or production.
