# Readiness Assessment Framework v1

## Purpose

This framework consolidates constitutional evidence into readiness judgments before Moon OS enters Today MVP Proof.

## Categories

Each category is assessed by:

- Evidence
- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Risk Level
- Assessment

Risk levels:

- Low
- Moderate
- High
- Critical

## Foundation Completeness

Evidence:

LAW, KNOWLEDGE, GRAPH, and METRICS layers exist and are documented.

Strengths:

Clear constitutional laws, continuity knowledge domains, graph ontology, and metric definitions.

Weaknesses:

No live data ingestion or production implementation yet.

Risk Level:

Low

Assessment:

Complete for limited proof.

## Constitutional Completeness

Evidence:

Continuity Director, Awareness QA, Trust QA, Task Writer, Today Surface Contract.

Strengths:

Reasoning authorities have scoped powers and explicit prohibitions.

Weaknesses:

Authorities are documented, not implemented as executable services.

Risk Level:

Moderate

Assessment:

Complete for constitutional proof, not production.

## Reasoning Readiness

Evidence:

Benchmark execution and audit traces.

Strengths:

20 scenarios passed; 5 audited traces credible.

Weaknesses:

No automated execution.

Risk Level:

Moderate

Assessment:

Ready for constrained Today MVP proof.

## Trust Readiness

Evidence:

Trust Governance, Trust QA, audit of Old Sensitive Memory and Fresh Start Protection.

Strengths:

Sensitive recall and active boundaries are rejected.

Weaknesses:

Future implementation must preserve consent logic exactly.

Risk Level:

Moderate

Assessment:

Ready for limited proof with strict guardrails.

## Attention Readiness

Evidence:

Attention Constitution, Awareness QA, Attention Overload audit.

Strengths:

Suppression, deferral, prioritization, and silence are supported.

Weaknesses:

Needs future stress testing at larger scale.

Risk Level:

Moderate

Assessment:

Ready for limited proof.

## Translation Readiness

Evidence:

Task Writer, artifact model, examples, benchmark artifacts.

Strengths:

Artifacts preserve why, why now, evidence, uncertainty, and trust notes.

Weaknesses:

Human readability not yet pilot-tested.

Risk Level:

Moderate

Assessment:

Ready for constrained proof.

Risk Level:

Low

Risk Level:

Risk Level:

Moderate

Assessment:

Sufficient for constrained Today MVP proof.

## Operational Readiness

Strengths:

High
