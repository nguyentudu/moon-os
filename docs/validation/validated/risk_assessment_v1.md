# Risk Assessment v1

## Purpose

Identify the largest risks remaining before Today MVP Proof.

## Biggest Continuity Risk

Description:

Missing hidden abandonment or underweighting commitment risk.

Impact:

Important continuity concerns may remain invisible.

Likelihood:

Moderate.

Mitigation:

Retest hidden abandonment and forgotten commitment scenarios during Today MVP Proof.

## Biggest Attention Risk

Description:

Over-surfacing valid but low-value awareness items.

Impact:

Moon Today may become noisy or attention-costly.

Likelihood:

Moderate.

Mitigation:

Preserve Awareness QA suppression and fewer-items rule.

## Biggest Trust Risk

Description:

Surfacing sensitive or old memory without explicit permission.

Impact:

Trust rupture.

Likelihood:

Moderate in future implementation if consent gates are weakened.

Mitigation:

Treat Trust QA rejection as a hard gate and retest Old Sensitive Memory and Fresh Start Protection.

## Biggest Translation Risk

Description:

Artifacts may overstate certainty or imply action pressure.

Impact:

User agency and trust may be weakened.

Likelihood:

Moderate.

Mitigation:

Require confidence, uncertainty, trust notes, and non-directive wording.

## Biggest Presentation Risk

Description:

Today Runtime may display items without preserving all required explanations.

Impact:

Unexplained awareness or hidden uncertainty.

Likelihood:

Low for current contract, moderate for future UI.

Mitigation:

Enforce Today Item Definition and Visibility Rules.

## Biggest Validation Risk

Description:

Scenario suite is not yet adversarial or longitudinal.

Impact:

Gaps may appear under real-world complexity.

Likelihood:

Moderate.

Mitigation:

Add adversarial and multi-day validation before pilots.

## Biggest Audit Risk

Description:

Only 5 of 20 scenarios were audited.

Impact:

Some benchmark assumptions may remain insufficiently traced.

Likelihood:

Moderate.

Mitigation:

Audit more scenarios before internal pilot.
