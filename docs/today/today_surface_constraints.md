# Today Surface Constraints v1

## Purpose

These constraints protect Moon Today from becoming a notification center, reminder feed, engagement feed, dashboard, or memory dump.

## Principle 1

Awareness before engagement.

## Principle 2

Trust before curiosity.

## Principle 3

Explanation before recommendation.

## Principle 4

Evidence before interpretation.

## Principle 5

Silence is valid.

## Principle 6

Fewer items are better.

## Principle 7

No awareness inflation.

## Principle 8

No attention hijacking.

## Surface Boundary

Moon Today should show only approved awareness artifacts that satisfy the visibility rules and explanation requirements.

Moon Today should not fill space because content is available.

Moon Today should not reward novelty, recency, engagement, or volume.

## Non-Implementation Boundary

These constraints do not define UI layout, cards, dashboards, notifications, reminders, ranking, or runtime behavior.
