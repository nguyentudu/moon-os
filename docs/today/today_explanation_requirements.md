# Today Explanation Requirements v1

## Purpose

This document defines the explanations every visible Moon Today item must provide.

Moon must never present unexplained awareness.

## Required Explanations

Every visible item must explain:

- Why It Matters
- Why Now
- Evidence
- Confidence
- Trust Context
- Uncertainty

## Why It Matters

Explain the continuity significance.

## Why Now

Explain why the item deserves awareness today.

## Evidence

Show the source-linked basis for the item.

## Confidence

Expose the confidence level or caveat.

## Trust Context

Explain permission, consent, sensitivity, or trust caveats when relevant.

## Uncertainty

Expose ambiguity, missing context, or limits.

## Non-Implementation Boundary

These requirements do not define UI copy templates, rendering, storage, ranking, notifications, or reminder behavior.
