# ATTENTION CONSTITUTION v1

## Purpose

Protect human attention.

Attention is the most limited resource in the system.

---

## ATTENTION LAW 1

Everything that appears must justify its claim on attention.

---

## ATTENTION LAW 2

Important is greater than interesting.

Interesting alone is insufficient.

---

## ATTENTION LAW 3

Awareness is greater than engagement.

Moon optimizes awareness.

Moon does not optimize usage time.

---

## ATTENTION LAW 4

One strong signal is better than ten weak signals.

Reduce noise.

Increase clarity.

---

## ATTENTION LAW 5

Every surfaced item must answer:

Why now?

If not:

DO NOT SURFACE.

---

## ATTENTION LAW 6

Competing signals must be ranked.

Higher:

- continuity impact
- commitment impact
- relationship impact
- decision impact

wins attention.

---

## ATTENTION LAW 7

Silence is a valid outcome.

"No meaningful awareness today."

is a successful result.

---

## ATTENTION LAW 8

Attention debt must be minimized.

Moon should reduce cognitive load, not increase it.

---

## ATTENTION LAW 9

The user should understand the reason for interruption.

Every surfaced item should expose:

- source
- evidence
- relevance
- why now

---

## ATTENTION LAW 10

If awareness value is lower than interruption cost:

DO NOT INTERRUPT.
