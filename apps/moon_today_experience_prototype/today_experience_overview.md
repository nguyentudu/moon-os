# Today Experience Prototype Overview v1

## Purpose

Create a tiny human-reviewable Today experience from the existing 5 approved awareness artifacts.

## Scope

This prototype renders exactly 5 existing awareness artifacts from:

- ../moon_today_proof/awareness_artifact_gallery.md

## Experience Intent

The experience should feel like morning awareness, not a productivity feed.

It is calm, minimal, and non-dashboard.

## Non-Goals

- No production app
- No ingestion
- No agents
- No notifications
- No login
- No new artifacts
- No new reasoning
- No new claims

## Human Review Use

Human reviewers can open index.html and evaluate whether the artifacts are understandable, relevant, trustworthy, and worth seeing.
