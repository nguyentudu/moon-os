# Reviewer Instructions v1

## Purpose

Guide human reviewers through the Moon Today Experience Prototype.

## How To Review

Open index.html in a browser.

Read the page as if it were a quiet morning awareness surface.

Evaluate only the experience and the artifacts shown.

Do not evaluate Moon architecture, internal authorities, or implementation plans.

## Review Questions

- Does this feel calm?
- Does this feel like awareness rather than a productivity feed?
- Are the five items understandable?
- Is the evidence visible enough?
- Is the why-now explanation clear?
- Are trust notes visible and useful?
- Does the page avoid pressure, urgency, and engagement mechanics?
- Would this be ready for 3-5 human reviewers?

## Constraints To Check

- Exactly 5 awareness artifacts are rendered.
- No notifications.
- No gamification.
- No fake data.
- No real user data.
- No login.
- No ingestion.
- No agents.
